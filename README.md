# exoplanet---eda
"Exploratory Analysis on the Open Exoplanet Catalogue datset containing 3584 confirmed and candidates exoplanets.
# what i explored:
missing value analysis across 25 columns
Data cleaning and pre_processing
Data discovery methods distribution
Exoplanets discovery over time
Planet mass vs radius relationship
Orbital period distribution
Host star Temperature vs planet surface temperature
Correlation  heatmap
#Key findings:
Transit methods accounts for 75% of all discoveries.
Kepler mission caused an explosion of discoveries after 2009.
KOI-55 b is the hottest planet at 7112K, hotter than our sun.
Orbital periods and semi major axis correlation of 0.97 confirms Kepler's third law.
#Tools used:
Python, Pandas, Matplolib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook
